> **Tagline:** Reveal how every change stacks up.

`DV_Waterfall` showcases the step-by-step journey from opening values to final totals. Positive and negative deltas, running totals, and optional labels are all rendered with theme-aware colors so stakeholders can see exactly where momentum shifts.

## Why it delivers

- **Purpose-built storytelling.** Feed a single series of deltas via `add_series` and the control automatically computes the running total, including opening and final bars.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Waterfall.xml†L1-L53】
- **Color-coded clarity.** Customize rise, fall, and total hues with `set_rise_color`, `set_fall_color`, and `set_total_color`, or leave them to the active `DVTheme` for instant cohesion.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Waterfall.xml†L53-L86】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L5-L86】
- **Chart-wide intelligence.** Because it inherits DVChart and DVComponent, you gain overlays, theming, tooltip toggles, and consistent formatting out of the box.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L5-L134】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】

## Launch in minutes

1. **Name the steps** – `set_categories(PackedStringArray([...]))` lists each change in order; “Total” is appended automatically for you.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Waterfall.xml†L26-L43】
2. **Feed the deltas** – `add_series("Δ Revenue", PackedFloat32Array([...]))` handles all the math behind the scenes.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Waterfall.xml†L26-L53】
3. **Control spacing** – `set_bar_gap_px(pixels)` balances density across dashboards, while `set_show_value_labels(true)` adds on-bar storytelling.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Waterfall.xml†L53-L86】
4. **Format with flair** – `set_value_format(mode)` and DVComponent’s decimal controls keep labels crisp and on-message.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Waterfall.xml†L53-L86】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L65-L90】

## API highlights

- `set_rise_color(color: Color)` / `set_fall_color(color: Color)` / `set_total_color(color: Color)` – ensure every viewer understands wins, hits, and final impact at a glance.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Waterfall.xml†L53-L86】
- `set_show_value_labels(enabled: bool)` – overlay numeric context exactly where viewers need it.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Waterfall.xml†L53-L86】
- `set_index_range(start: int, end: int)` – zoom into specific segments while maintaining accurate totals.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L248-L268】
- `set_series_color(index: int, color: Color)` – swap palettes on the fly to match your brand or highlight a scenario.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L287-L296】

## DVComponent & DVChart advantages

WaterfallView inherits animation timing, tooltip toggles, pixel snapping, and selection support, plus the overlay system shared by every DVChart. Hook `point_clicked` or `point_hovered` to cross-filter other widgets, and use `set_padding`/`set_margin` to slot the chart into any layout with zero friction.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L25-L104】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L205-L317】

WaterfallView makes the financial, resource, or narrative journey crystal clear—perfect for executive briefings, economy dashboards, or dramatic mission recaps.
