> **Tagline:** Headlines that rally the team.

`DV_KPI_Tile` turns a single metric into a hero moment. Titles, primary values, delta arrows, subtitles, footnotes, icons, and sparklines are all part of the package—styled automatically by the active `DVTheme` and powered by DVComponent’s layout intelligence.

## Why it wins dashboards

- **Instant storytelling.** Combine `set_title`, `set_value`, `set_delta`, and `set_subtitle` to communicate performance in seconds.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L1-L53】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L91-L142】
- **On-brand visuals.** Theme-driven typography, corner radius, animation timing, and delta colors keep every tile visually consistent across your product.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L5-L86】
- **Interactive by design.** Hook `tile_clicked` to open detail panels, switch scenes, or spotlight supporting charts the moment curiosity strikes.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L143-L150】

## Ship lightning-fast KPIs

1. **Drop the node** – DVComponent supplies padding, frames, tooltips, and pixel snapping, so the tile is visually ready from frame one.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L25-L64】
2. **Set the narrative** – call `set_prefix`, `set_value`, `set_suffix`, and `set_delta` to build the main headline, plus `set_subtitle` and `set_footnote` for context.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L91-L124】
3. **Spark interest** – feed a `PackedFloat32Array` into `set_sparkline` and choose between autoscale or a fixed range with `set_sparkline_autoscale` / `set_sparkline_range` for perfectly framed trends.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L55-L90】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L125-L142】
4. **Lean on theming** – adjust accent colors globally through `DVTheme` or locally via `set_col_delta_pos` / `set_col_delta_neg` and `set_title_col` when a specific hue matters.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L91-L142】

## API highlights

- `set_icon(texture: Texture2D)` / `set_icon_size(px: int)` – marry metrics with brand or gameplay icons effortlessly.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L106-L138】
- `set_sparkline_autoscale(enabled: bool)` – toggle between auto-normalized or consistent axes when comparing multiple tiles.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L55-L90】
- `set_sparkline_range(min: float, max: float)` – align multiple sparklines to the same Y-range for trustworthy comparisons.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_KPI_Tile.xml†L73-L90】
- `set_value_format(mode: int)` / `set_format_decimals(count: int)` from DVComponent ensure all numbers speak the same language across your dashboards.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L65-L90】

KpiTile delivers the kind of high-impact KPI presentation that executives, analysts, and players adore—without sacrificing speed, flexibility, or polish.
