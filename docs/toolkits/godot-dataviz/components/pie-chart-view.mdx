> **Tagline:** Own the spotlight on share.

`DV_PieChart` makes categorical breakdowns irresistible. From donut callouts and leader lines to grouped “other” slices and hover expansion, every interaction is tuned for clarity—and thanks to DVChart and DVComponent you get overlays, theming, and formatting without extra work.

## Why marketers and designers love it

- **Flexible storytelling.** Flip between full pie, donut, or polar area styles with `set_inner_ratio`, `set_donut_cap`, and `set_polar_area` to match any brand moment.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L1-L137】
- **Audience-ready labels.** Choose the perfect mix of category, value, and percent with `set_label_mode`, decide placement via `set_label_inside`, and add leader lines with `set_label_leader_lines`.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L1-L168】
- **Interactive polish.** Hover expansion, slice sorting, and `slice_clicked`/`slice_hovered` signals keep dashboards alive and actionable.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L1-L190】

## Create unforgettable pies

1. **Define categories** – `set_categories(PackedStringArray([...]))` sets the stage for your distribution.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L18-L56】
2. **Feed a series** – `add_series("Users", PackedFloat32Array([...]))` populates the slices; use `set_active_series_index` to toggle between multiple series if needed.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L18-L137】
3. **Style the center** – `set_center_title(text)`, `set_center_subtitle(text)`, and `set_show_center_value(true)` turn the donut middle into a hero callout.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L56-L137】
4. **Guide the eye** – `set_slice_gap_deg(deg)`, `set_start_angle_deg(deg)`, `set_clockwise(bool)`, and `set_sort_mode(mode)` tailor flow and emphasis.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L56-L168】
5. **Group the noise** – `set_group_below(threshold)` automatically combines tiny slices into an “Other” segment for clean presentations.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L126-L168】

## API highlights

- `set_hover_expand_px(px: float)` – add subtle motion that draws attention without overwhelming.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L126-L168】
- `set_label_mode(mode: int)` – choose from raw value, percent, category mixes, or disable labels entirely.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L126-L190】
- `set_custom_center_value(text: String)` – supply bespoke messaging when numbers alone aren’t enough.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L56-L137】
- `set_series_color(index: int, color: Color)` and `set_series_overlays(...)` (from DVChart) let you control palette and analytics in tandem.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L205-L317】

## Built on DVChart and DVComponent

PieChartView inherits the same theming, formatting, and interaction backbone as every chart in the DataViz kit:

- Global palettes, padding, and animation timing from `DVTheme` and `DVComponent` mean instant cohesion across dashboards.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L5-L86】
- Data management helpers like `set_categories`, `add_series`, `set_index_range`, and overlay utilities from `DVChart` supercharge experimentation.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L5-L317】
- Signals like `point_clicked`/`point_hovered` (DVComponent) plus slice-specific events keep everything in sync with the rest of your UI.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L91-L122】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_PieChart.xml†L168-L190】

PieChartView gives you blockbuster-quality donut and pie visuals that convert data into belief—whether you’re presenting to investors or delighting players with live stats.
