> **Tagline:** Make every needle move matter.

`DV_Gauge` delivers broadcast-quality radial gauges for monitoring vitals, resource levels, or performance metrics. With animated values, configurable zones, and DVComponent’s theme integration, you can go from placeholder to polished instrument panel in minutes.

## Why it excites ops teams and players

- **Zone-driven storytelling.** Add qualitative ranges with `add_zone(label, color, stop)` and reorder them with `insert_zone` or `set_zones` to spotlight safe, warning, and critical bands.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L5-L107】
- **Highly legible ticks.** Dial in major/minor counts, lengths, thickness, and label formatting via `set_ticks`, `set_tick_len_px`, `set_tick_thickness`, and `set_tick_label_decimals` for cockpit-ready clarity.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L107-L170】
- **Reactive interactions.** Connect `value_changed`, `zone_entered`, and `clicked` to trigger alarms, achievements, or contextual tooltips the instant values shift.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L170-L205】

## Quick-start workflow

1. **Set the range** – `set_min_value(value)` and `set_max_value(value)` establish your domain, while `set_unit("°C")` or similar labels the center readout.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L107-L170】
2. **Shape the gauge** – adjust `set_sweep_deg(deg)`, `set_thickness_px(px)`, `set_round_caps(true)`, and `set_inner_ratio` (via donut mode) to match your visual language.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L107-L170】
3. **Define zones** – call `add_zone` in order or bulk-load dictionaries through `set_zones(zones_array)` for curated experiences.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L5-L132】
4. **Feed live data** – `set_value(number)` animates smoothly between readings and emits signals for downstream automation.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L107-L170】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L170-L205】

## API highlights

- `set_center_title(text)` / `set_center_subtitle(text)` – anchor the gauge with a branded heading or key label.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L107-L132】
- `set_show_zone_labels(enabled: bool)` / `set_show_center_value(enabled: bool)` – manage how much context appears inside the gauge.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L107-L170】
- `set_value_format_mode(mode: int)` / `set_custom_value_text(text)` – choose between raw numbers, percentages, or bespoke readouts.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L107-L170】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L205-L220】
- `find_zone_at_value(value: float)` – instantly check which band a value belongs to for automated color or behavior changes.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Gauge.xml†L33-L73】

## DVComponent advantages you already love

Because Gauge inherits DVComponent, you gain theme-driven padding, frames, animation timing, pixel snapping, and tooltip toggles without lifting a finger. Switch `DVTheme` resources at runtime via `DVThemeManager` and your gauges follow suit—perfect for day/night modes or faction-specific skins.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】

Gauge brings command-center chic to your dashboards, making live data as thrilling to watch as the game itself.
