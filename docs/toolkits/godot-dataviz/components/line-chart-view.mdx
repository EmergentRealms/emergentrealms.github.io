> **Tagline:** Make trends feel inevitable.

`DV_LineChart` is the premium way to narrate progress over time. Seamlessly blend multi-series lines, area fills, moving averages, and Bollinger Bands without writing a single shader. Because it inherits from `DVChart` and `DVComponent`, you get battle-tested data pipelines, zero-hassle theming, and delightful hover experiences right out of the box.

## Why developers fall for it

- **Expressive, flexible visuals.** Enable or disable point markers, fill individual series with `set_series_filled`, and dial in line widths for razor-sharp storytelling.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_LineChart.xml†L1-L67】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_LineChart.xml†L70-L100】
- **Analytics baked in.** SMA, EMA, and Bollinger overlays are one method call away thanks to the shared `DVChart` backbone.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L205-L317】
- **Theme-driven polish.** Palette seeds, typography, padding, and animation curves flow straight from your active `DVTheme`, so every chart matches the rest of the UI kit instantly.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L5-L86】

## Launch in four beats

1. **Name the moments** – `set_categories(PackedStringArray([...]))` defines the horizontal timeline.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_LineChart.xml†L14-L47】
2. **Pipe in series** – call `add_series("Downloads", PackedFloat32Array([...]))` or `add_series_with_overlays(...)` for instant analytics.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L205-L247】
3. **Light up overlays** – choose the perfect mix with `set_series_overlays`, `set_series_sma_window`, `set_series_ema_window`, and `set_series_bbands_params` per series.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L270-L317】
4. **Tune the feel** – `set_line_width`, `set_point_radius`, `set_show_points`, and `set_series_filled` let you balance clarity and drama.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_LineChart.xml†L70-L100】

## API highlights

- `set_index_range(start: int, end: int)` – present sliding windows without touching your raw data.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L248-L268】
- `set_value_format(mode: int)` / `set_format_decimals(count: int)` – lean on DVComponent formatting for consistent currency, percent, or compact readouts.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L65-L90】
- `set_legend_position(position: int)` – leverage `DVThemeChartStyle` defaults or override per chart for perfect layouts.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L5-L52】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeChartStyle.xml†L3-L30】
- `set_series_color(index: int, color: Color)` – spotlight strategic series without repainting everything else.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L287-L296】

## Shared DVComponent advantages

Every LineChartView benefits from:

- **Adaptive layout controls** like `set_padding`, `set_margin`, and `set_ui_scale` for dashboards that resize gracefully.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L45-L64】
- **Snappy interactions** with built-in pixel snapping, tooltip toggles, and `point_clicked` / `point_hovered` signals inherited from DVComponent.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L25-L104】
- **Instant theming** through `DVThemeManager` so switching between dark and light palettes is as simple as swapping the active `DVTheme`.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】

LineChartView makes it effortless to go from raw metrics to jaw-dropping motion that keeps players, producers, and partners glued to the screen.
