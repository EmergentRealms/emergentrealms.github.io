> **Tagline:** One switch, a perfectly restyled dashboard.

The DataViz UI Kit ships with a cohesive theming architecture so every chart, tile, and grid stays in sync. `DVTheme`, `DVThemeManager`, and the family of component substyles let you author palettes, typography, and behavior once, then broadcast updates across your entire experience in real time.

## The cast of theme heroes

### DVTheme — your central style bible
`DVTheme` packages global palette seeds, layout metrics, animation timing, and nested style resources for specific component families. Apply it to any `DVComponent` or set it as the active theme to instantly restyle frames, typography, spacing, and selection behavior across the kit.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L5-L118】

Key highlights:
- Derive cohesive palettes from primary, neutral, success, warning, error, and info seeds—complete with dark/light support and accessible contrast targets.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L31-L70】
- Configure component padding, margins, frame thickness, corner radius, pixel snapping, and animation timing so every widget feels handcrafted.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L15-L56】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】
- Plug in specialized substyles (`chart`, `gauge`, `pie`, `radar`) to guarantee category-specific controls share the same polish.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L56-L118】

### DVThemeManager — the broadcast tower
`DVThemeManager` is the autoload node that stores the active theme and notifies every connected component when it changes. Toggle between day/night palettes, faction skins, or seasonal promos with a single assignment and watch the entire dashboard update instantly.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】

Power moves:
- Call `set_active_theme(theme)` to restyle live dashboards without rebuilding scenes.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L19-L33】
- Ship a curated default by filling `default_theme_path` and letting `load_default_theme()` initialize your brand look on startup.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】

### DVThemeChartStyle — chart-wide cohesion
`DVThemeChartStyle` defines defaults for every `DVChart` descendant: axis visibility, grid appearance, legend placement, value labels, and fallback palettes. Tune it once and your bar, line, pie, and waterfall visuals instantly harmonize.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeChartStyle.xml†L3-L44】

Highlights:
- Switch axes, grid, and built-in tooltips on or off globally.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeChartStyle.xml†L15-L34】
- Provide a brand palette and legend position that every new series inherits by default.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeChartStyle.xml†L31-L44】

### DVThemeGaugeStyle — instrument perfection
`DVThemeGaugeStyle` shapes every `DV_Gauge`: arc thickness, rounded caps, tick lengths, label padding, and whether the center value or zone labels appear. Align multiple gauges effortlessly without duplicating configuration.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeGaugeStyle.xml†L3-L39】

### DVThemePieStyle — magnetic share breakdowns
`DVThemePieStyle` sets donut thickness, label behavior, hover expansion, slice sorting, and grouping thresholds so all pies tell the same story. Flip between classic pies, polished donuts, or polar-area charts with a single resource tweak.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemePieStyle.xml†L3-L66】

### DVThemeRadarStyle — consistent capability maps
`DVThemeRadarStyle` ensures radar graphs share the same grid density, label margin, outline weight, and point sizing. Toggle grid rings, rotate labels, or hide points globally to match the mood of your analytics suite.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeRadarStyle.xml†L3-L40】

## Workflow: from palette concept to shipped experience

1. **Author a DVTheme resource.** In the inspector, set palette seeds, layout metrics, and attach tailored substyle resources (chart/gauge/pie/radar). Save it as a `.tres` or `.res` asset.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L5-L118】
2. **Register DVThemeManager as an autoload.** Add it to Project Settings → AutoLoad so `DVThemeManager.get_singleton()` is available everywhere.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】
3. **Load your default look.** Point `default_theme_path` to the resource and call `load_default_theme()` (or let `_ready()` do it) to initialize your dashboards instantly.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】
4. **Connect components.** Every DVComponent already listens for theme changes when `visual/theme_connected` is true, so they auto-refresh frames, fonts, and colors as soon as the manager broadcasts updates.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L16-L104】
5. **Swap at runtime.** Create alternate DVTheme resources for day/night or seasonal events and call `DVThemeManager.set_active_theme(new_theme)` to restyle everything live—no scene reloads required.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】

## Why your studio will love this system

- **Consistency without repetition.** Author one theme and let the kit propagate colors, padding, and animation timing across dozens of components automatically.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L5-L118】
- **Rapid experimentation.** Swap palettes in the editor or at runtime to test new looks, A/B variants, or seasonal events instantly.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】
- **Premium polish out of the box.** Substyles ensure gauges, pies, radars, and charts speak the same visual language—no more manually tweaking dozens of nodes.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeGaugeStyle.xml†L3-L39】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemePieStyle.xml†L3-L66】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeRadarStyle.xml†L3-L40】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeChartStyle.xml†L3-L44】

With the DataViz Theme System, refreshing your entire analytics experience is as easy as swapping a resource—so you can focus on the data and the drama, not repetitive styling chores.
