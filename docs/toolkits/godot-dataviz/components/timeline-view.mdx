> **Tagline:** Orchestrate every beat of your story.

`DV_Timeline` makes it effortless to visualize quests, production schedules, combat phases, or live-service roadmaps. Built on DVComponent, it respects your theme, handles layout polish automatically, and gives you granular control over tracks, events, and time windows.

## Why storytellers and producers rely on it

- **Track anything.** Add named lanes with `add_track`, then populate them with `add_event` including color, label, and metadata for callbacks.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L5-L84】
- **Time-aware controls.** Toggle autoscaling, define manual ranges, and adjust grid steps so timelines stay readable at any zoom level.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L5-L180】
- **Engagement built in.** `event_clicked` and `event_hovered` fire with full context, making it a breeze to launch cutscenes, detail panels, or analytics overlays.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L180-L217】

## Build a rich timeline fast

1. **Create lanes** – `add_track("Main Story")` returns a track index for subsequent events.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L44-L84】
2. **Populate events** – `add_event(track, start, end, label, color, meta)` paints bars with optional payloads for downstream logic.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L15-L84】
3. **Set the viewport** – choose between `set_time_autoscale(true)` for hands-free scaling or `set_time_range(min, max)` when you need a curated focus.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L94-L156】
4. **Dial the grid** – `set_grid_step(seconds)` and `set_show_grid(enabled)` keep long timelines legible without manual tinkering.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L94-L168】
5. **Highlight the moment** – turn on `set_now_enabled(true)` and feed `set_now_time(value)` to spotlight where the action is right now.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L44-L156】

## API highlights

- `set_track_height(px: float)` / `set_track_gap(px: float)` – optimize density for dense project plans or cinematic overviews.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L156-L180】
- `set_colors(bg, frame, grid, track_fill, event_fill, event_text, now_line)` – align the timeline with your brand in one call.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L108-L156】
- `get_track_names()` and `get_track_count()` – sync companion widgets like legends or filters effortlessly.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L94-L126】
- `clear()` – reset tracks and events between views or scenarios.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_Timeline.xml†L66-L84】

## DVComponent advantages baked in

TimelineView inherits animation controls, selection toggles, padding, and theme responsiveness from DVComponent. That means consistent fonts, frames, and tooltip behaviors across every panel, plus numeric formatting helpers when you combine the timeline with other metrics or UI elements.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】
