> **Tagline:** Compare heroes at a glance.

`DV_RadarGraph` gives you a striking way to compare capability spreads, attribute builds, or faction strengths. It inherits DVComponent’s theming and layout intelligence while providing dedicated controls for grid layers, labels, fills, and live data updates.

## Why designers gravitate to it

- **Clear, configurable grids.** Choose ring counts, toggle spokes, and rotate labels toward the center to keep every stat legible.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L1-L98】
- **Dynamic styling.** Adjust fill, outline, and point colors—or let your active DVTheme set the palette via `set_use_theme_colors(true)`.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L99-L164】
- **Real-time ready.** Update values through `set_data_points` and react to `queue_redraw()` to animate live progressions or randomization showcases.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L18-L78】

## Set up a radar in moments

1. **Define the axes** – `set_categories(PackedStringArray([...]))` labels each spoke of the graph.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L18-L78】
2. **Feed the values** – `set_data_points(PackedFloat32Array([...]))` aligns values to categories, mapping them between `set_min_value` and `set_max_value`.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L18-L98】
3. **Tune readability** – use `set_layer_count`, `set_label_margin`, `set_show_labels`, and `set_show_points` to match the density of your dashboard.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L99-L134】
4. **Style the brand** – control outline thickness, fill opacity, and point radius with `set_outline_thickness`, `set_fill_color`, and `set_point_radius`.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L134-L164】

## API highlights

- `set_rotate_labels_to_center(enabled: bool)` – keep text upright or tilt toward the hub for that premium infographic feel.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L99-L134】
- `set_show_grid(enabled: bool)` / `set_show_labels(enabled: bool)` – toggle supporting visuals depending on the story you want to tell.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L99-L134】
- `set_use_theme_colors(enabled: bool)` – instantly inherit palette decisions from your DVTheme or override per widget when highlighting special builds.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L134-L164】
- `set_label_margin(px: float)` – control breathing room for labels when embedding the radar in tight layouts.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_RadarGraph.xml†L99-L118】

## DVComponent advantages included

RadarGraphView ships with DVComponent’s padding, margins, animation timing, tooltip infrastructure, and theme connectivity. Swap UI scale, adjust frame settings, or change value formatting once and every radar in your project benefits immediately.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】

RadarGraphView turns stat comparisons into a visual spectacle, making it effortless to celebrate strengths, identify weaknesses, and energize your audience.
