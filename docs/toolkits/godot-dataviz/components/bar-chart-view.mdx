> **Tagline:** Turn comparisons into crowd-pleasers.

`DV_BarChart` turns categorical data into a story. Drop it into any dashboard and instantly deliver grouped or stacked columns, animated transitions, and built-in overlays that make trends leap off the screen. Every chart inherits the polished layout, typography, and tooltip system from `DVComponent`, so it feels right at home with the rest of the kit the moment you add it.

## What makes it irresistible

- **One control, every comparison.** Flip between grouped and stacked reads by toggling `set_stacked(true)` and tune gaps with `set_gaps(category_gap, bar_gap)` for pixel-perfect layouts.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_BarChart.xml†L5-L43】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_BarChart.xml†L56-L76】
- **Serious analytics on tap.** The shared `DVChart` engine powers category management, multi-series data, moving averages, and Bollinger overlays—no custom math required.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L5-L134】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L205-L317】
- **Theme-perfect out of the box.** Colors, spacing, and formatting flow straight from your active `DVTheme`, while DVComponent guarantees consistent padding, frames, pixel snapping, and rich tooltips.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L5-L104】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVTheme.xml†L5-L86】

## Plug-and-play workflow

1. **Bind categories** – `set_categories(PackedStringArray([...]))` defines the x-axis lanes once and keeps every series aligned.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_BarChart.xml†L16-L33】
2. **Stream your series** – `add_series("Revenue", PackedFloat32Array([...]))` or `add_series_with_overlays(...)` to light up SMA, EMA, and Bollinger bands in a single call.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L205-L247】
3. **Style with intent** – rely on palette-driven colors or call `set_series_color(index, color)` when a specific brand hue matters.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L287-L296】
4. **Delight users** – respond to `bar_clicked(series_idx, category_idx, value)` and `bar_hovered(...)` signals to launch drill-downs or reveal insights in context.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_BarChart.xml†L68-L76】

## Essential API highlights

- `set_gaps(category_gap: float, bar_gap: float)` – fine-tune spacing for clustered dashboards or hero widgets.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_BarChart.xml†L48-L55】
- `set_stacked(enabled: bool)` – swap between grouped comparisons and additive stories without touching data.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DV_BarChart.xml†L58-L62】
- `set_index_range(start: int, end: int)` – zoom into recent periods while the chart keeps overlays in sync.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L248-L268】
- `set_value_format(mode: int)` / `set_format_decimals(count: int)` – align numeric output with your brand voice using DVComponent formatting helpers.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L65-L90】

## Built-in polish from DVComponent & DVChart

Every DataViz chart comes with:

- **Cohesive theming** via `DVThemeManager`—switch palettes at runtime and every bar, label, and tooltip updates instantly.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L16-L43】【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVThemeManager.xml†L3-L33】
- **Responsive layout controls** like `set_padding`, `set_margin`, and `set_ui_scale` to fit any container or device.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVComponent.xml†L45-L64】
- **Interactive overlays** powered by `set_series_overlays`, `set_series_sma_window`, `set_series_ema_window`, and `set_series_bbands_params` for pro-grade analytics without crunching numbers yourself.【F:docs/toolkits/godot-dataviz/doc_classes/DVChart.xml†L270-L317】

With BarChartView you ship comparisons that feel premium, perform flawlessly, and convert curious players into believers.
